WasteHunter

There's a better way to keep our community clean

  • Plastic Waste Collection

    We collect all forms of plastic wastes and individuals are issued points based on the quantity of waste presented. Accumulated points can be used to receive cash and other benefits

  • Request to Pickup

    Sort your waste, request for pickup, and a waste hunter agent will come receive the plastic wastes and allot points to you based on the quantity

  • Report illegal dump sites

    Help us keep your environment safe by reporting illegal waste dumps around you and get rewarded

We can all work together to achieve environmental sustainability
